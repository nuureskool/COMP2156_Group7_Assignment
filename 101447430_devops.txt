Comp 2156 DevOps for system administration is a course that introduces the principles of DevOps
in mordern IT enviroments. The course focuses on bridging the gap between software development
and system administration

in this course students learn how DevOps helps organizations deliver software faster and more
reliably. Topics include Git and (CI/CD), and infrastructure automation.

COMP 2156 also emphasizes hands-on learning through practical assignment projects.
By working with real-world tools and workflows students gain experience to prepare them for DevOps roles.
The skills learned by students will help them i future.
